Simple process
From prompt to scene in seconds
1

Write a prompt

Describe the mood, style, and energy you want. "A cosmic void with geometric pulse at 120BPM."

2

AI designs the scene

The Director agent breaks your prompt into a structured scene spec — tempo, palette, shapes, motion.

3

Pick a generator

Choose p5.js animation, 4-layer ASCII, or procedural audio. Each interprets the spec differently.

4

Share the result

Get a shareable URL and embed code. The scene runs standalone in any browser.
